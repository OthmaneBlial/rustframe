# Cold chain audit snapshot

The temperature alerts themselves are not the immediate problem. The issue is that the follow-up notes do not explain whether a breach was confirmed or dismissed.

## What to fix first

- Require one closure reason on every alert.
- Standardize the difference between sensor drift and confirmed exposure.
- Add the receiving bay location to the closure note.
