# Night shift interview with operations director

The operations director kept returning to one theme: the night team can execute, but it loses time waiting for decisions that should already be delegated.

## Friction points

- Exception approvals still require a daytime supervisor in too many cases.
- New leads are covering labor shortages and decision triage at the same time.
- Training content is written for the day shift cadence, not the actual night workflow.

## Immediate implication

The next experiment should focus on decision rights before it adds more headcount.
