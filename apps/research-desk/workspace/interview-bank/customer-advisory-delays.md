# Customer advisory on delivery delays

The advisory group reacted less to the total delay than to the vagueness of the second update. They wanted a reason they could repeat back to their own teams.

## Direct takeaways

- "Still investigating" reads as avoidance after the first message.
- Carrier language is useful only when paired with a concrete next checkpoint.
- Customers prefer one confident update over several cautious ones.
