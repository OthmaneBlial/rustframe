# Cross-dock reallocation brief

The current overflow lane is absorbing volume too late in the sort cycle. Moving it closer to Dock A reduces handoff time and removes one avoidable relay.

## Working assumptions

- Arrival variability holds inside the current twelve percent band.
- Team leads can rotate one experienced picker to the new lane during the pilot.
- The Friday inbound spike remains the worst-case scenario.

## What to validate this week

- Whether the pilot actually reduces queue depth before 18:30.
- Whether the new lane placement creates any pedestrian pinch points.
- Whether relabeling can happen without breaking the scanner flow.
